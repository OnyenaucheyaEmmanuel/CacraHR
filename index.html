About

Who We Are at CacraHR

Building Strong Organisations Through People

CacraHR is a human resources consulting and outsourcing company dedicated to helping organisations attract, develop and retain the right talent.

We support businesses at every stage of their people journey – from workforce planning and recruitment to onboarding, performance management and employee experience. Our team combines HR expertise with practical business understanding to deliver solutions that actually work on the ground.

Whether you are a growing startup or an established organisation, we design flexible HR structures, policies and processes that keep you compliant, reduce people risk and free your leaders to focus on strategy and execution.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked HR Questions

01

What types of HR services does CacraHR provide?

We offer recruitment and talent acquisition, outsourced HR and payroll, training and development, HR policy design, performance management frameworks and general HR advisory for organisations of different sizes.

02

Do you work with both small businesses and large organisations?

Yes. Our solutions are flexible and scalable. We support startups, SMEs and large enterprises by tailoring our HR services to their current stage, budget and growth plans.

03

Can CacraHR handle bulk or ongoing recruitment?

Absolutely. We manage high-volume recruitment campaigns as well as continuous hiring for frequently needed roles, ensuring a steady pipeline of qualified candidates.

04

How do you charge for HR services?

Our pricing depends on the scope of work. Some services are billed per project or per hire, while others use a monthly retainer model for ongoing HR support. We always provide a clear proposal before we start.

05

How quickly can we get started with CacraHR?

Once we understand your HR needs, we share a simple engagement plan. Many clients start with a consultation and can begin recruitment or HR setup within a few days.
